About Our CBG Plant

Our Compressed Bio Gas (CBG) Plant produces high-quality bio-methane gas from agricultural residue, organic waste, and biodegradable materials. The plant contributes to waste management, clean fuel production, and reduction of greenhouse gas emissions.

CBG Production Process

Feedstock Collection

Agricultural waste, press mud, cow dung, food waste, and other organic materials are collected.

Anaerobic Digestion

Organic waste is decomposed in digesters to produce raw biogas.

Gas Purification

Biogas is upgraded by removing CO₂, H₂S, and moisture to achieve CBG quality.

Compression & Storage

Purified bio-methane is compressed and stored for commercial use.
